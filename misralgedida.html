Cairo Governorate | Misr Al-Gedida (Helioplis)

We are the Heliopolis neighborhood, our place is a suburb in the northeast of Cairo, Egypt. The neighborhood was built by a Belgian company in the desert in 1910, in place of old Heliopolis, with wide streets and squares. We always strive to develop our services and activities to meet the citizens' interests and desires. We are not only satisfied with providing our services, but we strive to always involve citizens in developing the neighborhood and improving its services. We always care about the opinions of citizens in the political decision-making process in the neighborhood, and we provide all the mechanisms to support and develop their culture. Protecting the citizen is our responsibility, and protecting the neighborhood is our responsibility as partners in it.
